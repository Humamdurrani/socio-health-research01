# socio-health-research01
# Humam Zaman

**Researcher · Health Equity · AI Ethics · Medical Sociology**

I work at the intersection of healthcare equity, algorithmic justice, 
and the sociology of medicine — asking hard questions about who 
technology serves, who it harms, and why that follows existing lines 
of power and marginalization.

---

Research Focus

- **Health Inequities in Marginalized Communities** — examining how 
  structural and social forces shape unequal health outcomes across 
  populations globally
- **Algorithmic Bias in Clinical AI** — investigating how machine 
  learning systems trained on biased data reproduce and amplify 
  disparities in diagnosis, treatment, and care
- **Social Determinants & Data Justice** — exploring how data 
  collection, representation, and use either challenge or entrench 
  inequity in health systems

---

Approach

My work draws from sociology, medical ethics, and critical data 
studies. I believe rigorous research must be accountable to the 
communities it studies — not just the institutions that fund it.

I am building this space to share research, writing, frameworks, 
and open resources for anyone working at the crossroads of 
**health, AI, and social justice.**

---

What You'll Find Here

- Research notes and working papers
- Annotated reading lists and literature reviews
- Frameworks for ethical evaluation of health AI systems
- Datasets and references for equity-focused research
